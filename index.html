Our Services

Traditional Thai Massage

Nuad Bo Rarn, or Traditional Thai Massage, is an ancient form of bodywork that has roots in Ayrvedic medicine - the traditional medicine of India. It consists of deep compressions and full-body passive stretches to bring deep mental and physical relaxation to one's body.

Swedish Massage

A Swedish massage is the classical style of Western massage which uses lotion or oil to glide over the body. It incorporates a combination of 5 basic strokes: effleurage, petrissage, friction, vibration/shaking, and tapotement, to relieve muscle tension and promote relaxation.

Pre-Natal Massage

A woman's body changes dramatically throughout pregnancy, and new and increased levels of tension arise as a result. Prenatal massage is an incredibly effective method of treating the extra strain on the mother's body and mind.

Sports Massage

Sports massage consists of manipulation of soft tissue of the body, and focuses on areas that are specific to the athlete's sport/activity. It can be used for pre-event preparation in order to warm the tissues up and 'awaken' them. Post-event massage is used for recovery, and can be performed immediately after your event/activity, or up to a few days after.
